## MailerLite lifecycle audit

- Ran a full live MailerLite audit across automations, groups, forms, and lifecycle subscriber state, with special focus on `1.1. Trialling Simplified Aug 2025 (15 Days)`.
- Core conclusion: Oviond's MailerLite problem is not mainly copy quality; it is lifecycle architecture, state hygiene, and activation logic.
- Key finding: the live trial onboarding flow is materially underperforming on clicks and activation, while the paying-customer onboarding flow is comparatively healthy.
- Key finding: lifecycle groups are dirty enough that trial, expired, active, and canceled contacts are sitting in the wrong journeys, which undermines automation accuracy.
- Recommended strategic fix in MailerLite: clean lifecycle state/routing first, replace the current 1.1 trial onboarding with a behavior-led activation sequence, rebuild the expired-trial flow by activation stage rather than blanket discounts, and consolidate reactivation clutter.
- Near-term operational alert from the audit: most trial users nearing expiry were still unactivated, especially lacking integrations and first reports.
