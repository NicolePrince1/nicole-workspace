# Asset Specs

Use these defaults unless the brief overrides them.

## Social defaults

- LinkedIn square post: 1080 x 1080
- LinkedIn landscape: 1200 x 627
- Instagram square: 1080 x 1080
- Instagram portrait: 1080 x 1350
- Story / Reel cover direction: 1080 x 1920

## Paid social defaults

- Meta square ad: 1080 x 1080
- Meta portrait ad: 1080 x 1350
- Meta story / reel ad: 1080 x 1920
- LinkedIn sponsored image: 1200 x 627

## Display starter set

Use only when a display brief appears and adapt the concept to smaller canvases:
- 300 x 250
- 336 x 280
- 728 x 90
- 160 x 600
- 300 x 600
- 320 x 50

## Web defaults

- Landing-page hero concept: design with a wide desktop composition first
- Also consider tablet/mobile crop survival
- Leave safe areas for headline, subhead, and CTA blocks

## Email defaults

- Email banner concept: keep composition simple and center-safe
- Prefer shallow depth and low detail

## Placement rules

Before finalizing any prompt, note:
- final platform
- primary dimensions
- likely crop variations
- whether in-image text is required or should be added later
- whether the design needs transparent-background extraction or not

## Resizing strategy

Do not ask the generator to solve every placement at once. Create a primary master concept for the most important dimension, then adapt selectively for secondary formats.
