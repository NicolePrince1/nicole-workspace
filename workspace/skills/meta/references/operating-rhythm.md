# Meta operating rhythm for Oviond

## Daily

- Check yesterday and last 7 days for spend, clicks, LPVs, and primary KPI drift.
- Flag sudden CPA spikes, delivery drops, or platform imbalance.
- Watch active campaigns for accidental overspend or zero-conversion burn.

## Weekly

- Review campaign and ad set efficiency.
- Review ad-level winners/losers and identify creative fatigue.
- Check platform, age/gender, and country/device breakdowns.
- Compare current 7-day window to prior 7-day window.
- Separate scaling candidates from repair candidates.

## Monthly

- Re-read action types to catch new custom conversions or tracking changes.
- Review objective mix: traffic vs remarketing vs lower-funnel conversion campaigns.
- Reassess whether budget is aligned with actual conversion quality, not just click volume.
- Summarize learning into creative, landing-page, and targeting recommendations.

## Decision framework

When performance is weak, isolate the failure point in this order:
1. delivery and spend
2. click-through rate
3. landing-page view rate
4. primary conversion rate
5. economics by campaign/ad set/ad

This keeps diagnosis grounded instead of hand-wavy.
