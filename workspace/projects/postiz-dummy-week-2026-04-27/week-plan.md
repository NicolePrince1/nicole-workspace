## Safety note

Every scheduled item is clearly marked:

`DUMMY SCHEDULE TEST, REMOVE BEFORE PUBLISH`

This was deliberate so the system could be demonstrated without pretending the content was final or approved for real publication.

## Daily themes

### Monday
Reporting should not feel like a second job.

### Tuesday
Pretty dashboards do not create trust. Clean numbers do.

### Wednesday
Feature bloat is not a moat. Simple setup and low admin win.

### Thursday
AI helps, but it does not replace the operational layer.

### Friday
Retention starts with trust and low-friction delivery, not just pricing.
