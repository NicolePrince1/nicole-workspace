# Gleap customer sentiment sweep — 2026-06-02

Read-only sweep for launch positioning. No customer-visible actions taken.

## Dataset

- Source: Gleap v3 API.
- Recent sample: 500 most recently updated non-archived tickets, covering roughly 2026-05-12 to 2026-06-02 UTC.
- Filtered read: removed obvious internal QA, vendor/newsletter/social-notification noise, and focused on customer/support-relevant signal.
- Extra targeted searches: API, MCP, Claude, ChatGPT, AI reporting, white label, custom domain, email bounce, scheduled reports, data source, integration, template, dashboard.
- Important caveat: Gleap contains noisy inbound email/newsletter/vendor items, so counts are directional. The qualitative themes below are the stronger signal.

## Executive read

Customer tone is **practical, impatient, and trust-seeking**.

They are not mainly asking for a shiny new dashboard. They are asking:

- Can I trust the numbers?
- Can I connect the sources I need?
- Can my reports go out cleanly to clients?
- Can I see what failed?
- Can I use API/MCP/AI workflows with Oviond?
- Can setup/admin be less painful?

That supports the launch message: **Oviond is finally here as a simpler, more dependable agency reporting system — and it is AI/API-ready without locking agencies into one AI provider.**

## Main customer themes

### 1. Data trust and connector confidence

Repeated support signal around data-source and metric confidence:

- Meta/Facebook Ads metric mismatches versus Ads Manager.
- Blank or failing social widgets.
- Missing or deprecated Meta metrics.
- LinkedIn / TikTok / YouTube / GA-style data source questions.
- Integration API query errors.

**Messaging implication:** lead with reliability, not hype. “Rebuilt for cleaner reporting workflows, stronger connector foundations, and fewer reporting surprises.”

### 2. Report delivery confidence

Customers are asking about report email bounces, who received scheduled reports, PDF/download behavior, and logs.

**Messaging implication:** customer-facing delivery matters as much as dashboard creation. Use language like: “client-ready reports that go out cleanly, with clearer delivery visibility.”

### 3. API, MCP, and AI-readiness are real questions

Targeted Gleap searches found live/recent asks including:

- “Where can I access the API for Oviond?”
- “Does Oviond have an API?”
- Requests for API keys, client IDs, rate limits, and developer docs.
- MCP availability for Claude Code.
- MCP server not working.
- Integration API 500 errors.

**Messaging implication:** Chris is right — AI/API/MCP cannot be buried. But the right framing is not “AI chatbot.” It is: **use Oviond from ChatGPT, Claude, or the AI tools your agency already uses. No lock-in. Simple UI for humans; API/MCP for AI and automation workflows.**

### 4. Setup/admin friction

Recent tickets show ordinary agency ops work: adding/removing clients, extending trials, custom domains, account access, and client/report setup.

**Messaging implication:** the pricing/client-count message is valuable because it matches the way agencies think. “Pick your client count. Get the full reporting system.”

### 5. Dashboard/report polish

Common asks cluster around widgets, tables, dimensions, filters, templates, custom metrics, PDF/export, and report-view behavior.

**Messaging implication:** “finally simple” must mean operationally simple — not just a prettier UI. Avoid overclaiming; emphasize cleaner workflows and fewer moving parts.

### 6. White-label and custom domain remains a buying/trust signal

Searches showed recurring custom-domain and white-label requests/history.

**Messaging implication:** keep white-label delivery high in the hierarchy. For agencies, this is not a side feature; it is client trust infrastructure.

## Sentiment read

- **Negative/friction:** mostly where data, delivery, API, or setup paths are unclear or failing.
- **Request/question:** customers are asking “how do I?” and “is there a way?” more than ranting.
- **Positive/thankful:** support replies still generate appreciation; the human support layer is valuable.
- **Underlying emotion:** “please make this dependable enough that I do not have to babysit reporting.”

## Recommended launch answer

Use this as the customer-backed message:

> Oviond has been rebuilt to make agency reporting feel simple again: reliable client-ready reports, clearer setup, straightforward pricing by client count, and AI/API workflows you can use from ChatGPT, Claude, or whichever AI tool your agency already trusts.

## Copy angles pulled from the sentiment

- “Finally, agency reporting you do not have to babysit.”
- “Client-ready reports, clearer delivery, and fewer moving parts.”
- “Use Oviond from ChatGPT, Claude, or your preferred AI assistant — no AI-provider lock-in.”
- “Simple UI for your team. API and MCP for your AI workflows.”
- “Pick your client count. Get the full reporting system.”
- “Built for agencies that need reporting to be invisible, dependable, and done.”

## Do not overclaim

Avoid saying:

- “Every integration issue is solved.”
- “AI will do all your reporting.”
- “No setup required.”
- “Everything is fully automated.”

Safer claim:

> Rebuilt foundations, simpler workflows, clearer delivery, and AI/API-ready access — with support watching launch-week issues closely.
