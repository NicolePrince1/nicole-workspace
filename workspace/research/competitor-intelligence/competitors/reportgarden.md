## ReportGarden

Last verified: 2026-05-07
Priority: Tier 2
Category: Direct agency reporting
Website: https://reportgarden.com

## Quick read

- **What they are:** Marketing dashboard/reporting software for agencies and in-house teams with reports, dashboards, data blending, SEO/PPC/social/franchise reporting.
- **Best-fit customer:** Freelancers and smaller/mid-size agencies looking for lower-entry client reporting with broad integrations.
- **Core promise:** Simple, beautiful multi-channel reports and dashboards; automation and customization.
- **Main Oviond relevance:** Lower-cost direct option, but with client caps and fair-usage mechanics.

## Positioning and messaging

- Homepage promise: “Create simple & beautiful multi-channel ... marketing reports.”
- Pricing promise: Plans starting from $75/month.
- Target audience: Digital marketing agencies and in-house teams.
- Key language/claims: 1M reports, 100,000+ dashboards, 7000+ agencies, 100+ integrated data sources, white-label/share/automated reports.
- Emotional angle: Save time, customize reports, support agency decision-making.

## Pricing and packaging

- **Pricing model:** Plan tiers by clients, with fair-usage/data-source billing logic.
- **Entry price:** Standard: **$89/mo monthly** or **$75/mo annual** for up to 5 clients. Professional: **$149/mo monthly** or **$125/mo annual** for up to 10 clients. Custom: contact sales.
- **Scaling metric:** Clients, plus data sources counted toward usage/fair usage.
- **Free trial/demo:** 14-day free trial, no credit card.
- **Annual-pressure:** Annual discounts available.
- **Key gates:**
  - Clients/accounts: Standard up to 5 clients; Professional up to 10; Custom thereafter.
  - Data sources/connectors: 100+ integrated data sources; only data sources used to create reports count toward billing usage.
  - Dashboards/reports: Unlimited reports/dashboards on public plans.
  - White label: White-label reports and sharing; “Whitelabel, share, and automated all your reports.”
  - Custom domains: Not verified.
  - Users/seats: Not verified.
  - AI: “InsightHub” appears in nav; AI posture not fully verified.
  - API/MCP: Not verified.
  - Refresh rate: Live integrations; scheduling/automation implied.
  - Support/implementation: Dedicated onboarding POC on Professional; white-glove/custom integrations on Custom.

## Product and capability map

- Dashboards/reports: Customizable dashboards and automated reporting.
- Templates: Premium templates gated to Professional.
- Integrations: 100+ integrated data sources; Google Ads, GA/GA4, Facebook Ads/Insights, Instagram, GSC, GBP, Drive visible.
- White label/client portals: White-label reports, public URL sharing.
- Data transformation/blending: Cross-platform reporting with data blending and custom metrics.
- Automation/scheduling: End-to-end report automation.
- AI/narratives/insights: InsightHub likely AI/insight layer; needs separate verification.
- API/docs/MCP: Not verified.
- Onboarding/setup: Free trial; demo; onboarding POC on Professional.

## Strengths

- Lower public entry point for agencies.
- Broad integrations and data blending.
- Strong customization and agency case-study proof.
- Simple public tiering.

## Weaknesses / exploitable gaps

- Client caps are low at entry/Professional.
- Fair-usage/data-source rules introduce hidden complexity.
- Custom integrations/white-glove services are custom-tier.
- Less modern AI/API positioning than Whatagraph/TapClicks/NinjaCat.
