## Swydo

Last verified: 2026-05-07
Priority: Tier 1
Category: Direct agency reporting
Website: https://www.swydo.com

## Quick read

- **What they are:** Agency reporting platform with one plan, all features included, and usage pricing by active data source.
- **Best-fit customer:** Agencies that value full feature access and can forecast active source counts.
- **Core promise:** One plan, unlimited users/dashboards/reports, all features included.
- **Main Oviond relevance:** Packaging is philosophically close to Oviond’s “all features included” stance, but the scaling metric is data sources rather than clients.

## Positioning and messaging

- Pricing promise: “One Plan (Includes All Features) Get Unlimited Users + 10 Data Sources.”
- Target audience: Agencies managing client reporting.
- Key language/claims: No client or seat limits; AI summaries; full white-labeling; 32+ integrations; personal/live support.
- Emotional angle: Predictable feature access, fewer hidden fees, agency-friendly scaling.

## Pricing and packaging

- **Pricing model:** One plan + volume pricing by active data source.
- **Entry price:** **€69/mo or $69/mo** including first 10 data sources; annual price shown as €62/mo with 10% saving.
- **Scaling metric:** Active data sources. 11–100: $/€4.50 each; 101–500: $/€3 each; 501+: $/€2 each.
- **Free trial/demo:** 14-day full trial, no credit card; demo optional.
- **Annual-pressure:** 10% annual saving, but monthly available.
- **Key gates:**
  - Clients/accounts: No client limits.
  - Data sources/connectors: Main billing metric; every account connected to an integration counts as one data source.
  - Dashboards/reports: Unlimited dashboards and reports.
  - White label: Full white-labeling and branding tools included.
  - Custom domains: Custom domain hosting included.
  - Users/seats: Unlimited users.
  - AI: AI report summaries included with monthly credits; overage can be controlled by spend limit.
  - API/MCP: No public API posture found in fetched source.
  - Refresh rate: Data source health/connectivity checks; exact refresh not verified.
  - Support/implementation: Email, in-app chat, customer success specialist, personalized onboarding, 1:1 demo sessions included.

## Product and capability map

- Dashboards/reports: Shareable live dashboards, branded PDFs, drag-and-drop builder.
- Templates: Pre-built, custom, dynamic templates; 14 languages.
- Integrations: 32+ integrations plus Google Sheets import.
- White label/client portals: Client portal access, custom links, custom domains, branded email/report styling.
- Data transformation/blending: Custom metrics, combined ad data sources, filters, goal tracking.
- Automation/scheduling: Report scheduling, delivery logs, auto-resend, alerts.
- AI/narratives/insights: AI-powered report summaries with credits.
- API/docs/MCP: Not a visible strength.
- Onboarding/setup: Strong support/onboarding story.

## Strengths

- “One plan/all features” is the cleanest packaging among direct competitors.
- Unlimited users, dashboards, reports, and clients directly answers agency concerns.
- Strong support/onboarding included.
- White label and custom domains are included, not obviously gated.

## Weaknesses / exploitable gaps

- Data-source pricing can escalate quickly for multi-channel agencies; 10 clients with Google Ads + Facebook Ads = 20 data sources, per their own FAQ.
- Agencies do not naturally budget by “active data sources”; they budget by client count.
- Smaller connector list than some larger platforms.
- AI credit model adds a usage dimension.

## Oviond counter-position

“Swydo’s all-features-included posture is strong. Oviond can beat it on the scaling unit: agencies should not need to count every platform account across every client to forecast reporting cost.”
