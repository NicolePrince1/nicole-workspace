## Metrics Watch

Last verified: 2026-05-07
Priority: Watchlist / Tier 2 for email-reporting niche
Category: Automated email reporting / alerts
Website: https://www.metricswatch.com

## Quick read

- **What they are:** Automated client reporting via email, plus separate real-time data monitoring/alerts product.
- **Best-fit customer:** Agencies or marketers wanting reports delivered directly to client inboxes, especially without needing a client portal.
- **Core promise:** Simple transparent pricing for automated email reports and data reliability alerts.
- **Main Oviond relevance:** Simplicity threat at the lightweight end; not a full portal/dashboard competitor.

## Positioning and messaging

- Pricing promise: “Simple, Transparent Pricing.”
- Target audience: Agencies/client reporting and teams monitoring GA4/data issues.
- Key language/claims: Automated client reporting, unlimited data sources, email delivery, reports sold separately from alerts.
- Emotional angle: Save hours, get reports in the inbox, catch data issues before they cost money.

## Pricing and packaging

- **Pricing model:** Reports plan by report count; alerts sold separately by alert count/detection speed/channels.
- **Entry price:** Reports Startup: **$49/mo** up to 2 reports. Pro: **$149/mo** up to 25 reports. Agency: **$399/mo** up to 100 reports. Alerts start at $79/mo.
- **Scaling metric:** Reports; alerts separately.
- **Free trial/demo:** 14-day free trial, no credit card.
- **Annual-pressure:** Annual billing gets 2 months free.
- **Key gates:**
  - Clients/accounts: Each report is a unique combination of data sources and recipients; client count maps indirectly to report count.
  - Data sources/connectors: Unlimited data sources in reports plans.
  - Dashboards/reports: Email reports; report count is the gate.
  - White label: Not verified from pricing page.
  - Custom domains: Not verified.
  - Users/seats: Not verified.
  - AI: Alerts include remediation context and reliability reports; no broad AI-reporting posture verified.
  - API/MCP: Not verified.
  - Refresh rate: Alerts detection speed 15/10/5 minutes depending on plan; reporting schedule not detailed on pricing page.
  - Support/implementation: Priority support on Pro/Agency; custom integrations on Agency; enterprise alerts include SSO/RBAC/SLA.

## Product and capability map

- Dashboards/reports: Email-delivered automated reports; not portal-led.
- Templates: Not verified.
- Integrations: Unlimited data sources claimed; custom integrations on Agency.
- White label/client portals: Not a prominent claim in fetched pricing.
- Data transformation/blending: Unique combination of data sources/recipients defines report; deeper blending not verified.
- Automation/scheduling: Core email report automation.
- AI/narratives/insights: Monitoring/remediation context more than report-narrative AI.
- API/docs/MCP: Not verified.
- Onboarding/setup: Trial-first.

## Strengths

- Very simple lightweight reporting model.
- Unlimited data sources is attractive.
- Email-native delivery meets many client-reporting workflows.
- Separate alerts product can appeal to analytics reliability buyers.

## Weaknesses / exploitable gaps

- Report-count pricing can penalize agencies with multiple clients/frequencies/recipient sets.
- Less robust client portal/white-label/custom-domain posture than direct agency platforms.
- Lower visible integration/visual-reporting depth than AgencyAnalytics/Whatagraph/DashThis/Swydo.
- Alerts pricing is separate, adding second-product complexity.

## Oviond counter-position

“Metrics Watch is great for inbox-first reporting. Oviond should position as the simple full reporting platform: email delivery when needed, but with client dashboards/white label/API included in a broader agency workspace.”
