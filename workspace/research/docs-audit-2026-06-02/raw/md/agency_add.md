# Add Project

> Create an account-level agency report or dashboard.

An **agency report** is a project that lives at the account level — no client scope. Use them for internal dashboards, pitch decks, or cross-client rollups.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Agency screen">
    Click **Agency** in the left sidebar. This lists every account-level project.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click + New Agency Report">
    Opens the create dialog — no client picker since agency reports aren't scoped to a client.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name your project">
    The name appears on the shared link, in PDF exports, and in automation email subjects.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick the type">
    **Report** (paged) or **Dashboard** (single-scroll).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start blank or from a template">
    Templates work the same way they do for client projects — see [Apply Template](/templates/apply).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create">
    Oviond opens the editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Data sources

Agency reports pull from the agency's **shared integrations** — data sources connected at the account level rather than to a specific client. Connect them under [Data Sources](/data-sources/list) and they become available to every agency report.
