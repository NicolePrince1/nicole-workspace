# Update Project

> Rename and configure settings on an agency report.

Agency-report settings cover everything outside the canvas — name, date range defaults, and PDF export options. There's no client reassignment since agency reports aren't scoped to a client.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the agency report">
    From the Agency screen, click the report you want to edit.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Project Settings">
    Click the **Settings** icon in the editor or choose **Project Settings** from the three-dot menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Rename">
    Edit the **Project name** field. The new name appears on the shared link and PDF exports.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the default date range">
    Pick a fixed range or a relative range (e.g. *Last 30 Days*) — every widget that uses the project-level date inherits this.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure PDF export">
    Under **PDF**, set paper size, orientation, and any header / footer content.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to apply.
  </Step>
</Steps>
