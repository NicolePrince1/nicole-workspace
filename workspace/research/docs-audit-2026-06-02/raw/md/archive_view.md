# View Archive

> Browse every soft-deleted client, project, and media item.

Everything you delete in Oviond is **soft-deleted first** — moved to the Archive rather than permanently removed. The Archive view shows:

* Archived **projects** (reports and dashboards)
* Archived **clients** (with their projects nested)
* Archived **media** (images and files)

From here you can **restore** an item back to its original location, or **permanently delete** it to free up quota. Items older than the retention window (90 days by default) are purged automatically.
