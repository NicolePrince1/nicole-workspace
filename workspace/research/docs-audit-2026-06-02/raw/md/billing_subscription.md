# Subscription

> Change plan, update payment method, or cancel your Oviond subscription.

Oviond uses Stripe to handle all subscription billing. You can manage your plan, payment method, and invoices directly from your account settings or through the Stripe customer portal.

## Access the billing portal

Go to **Settings → Billing** and click **Manage Billing**. This opens the Stripe customer portal in a new tab, where you can:

* Update your credit card or payment method
* View and download past invoices
* Cancel or change your subscription

## Update your payment method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the billing portal">
    Go to **Settings → Billing → Manage Billing**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Payment Methods">
    In the Stripe portal, select **Payment Methods** and click **Add payment method**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter card details">
    Enter your new card number, expiry, and CVC, then click **Add**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set as default">
    Click **Set as default** next to your new card to use it for future charges.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## View and download invoices

In the Stripe billing portal, click **Invoice history** to see all past payments. Click any invoice to view or download a PDF receipt.

## Cancel your subscription

In the Stripe billing portal, click **Cancel plan**. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, your account switches to a limited free tier.

<Warning>
  Canceling your subscription does not delete your data immediately. However, if your usage exceeds free-tier limits, some features will become inaccessible until you resubscribe.
</Warning>
