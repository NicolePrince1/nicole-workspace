# Test Automation

> Send an automation immediately to verify its content.

A **test send** bypasses the schedule and emails the automation to you (or a test recipient) right now. Use it after creating or editing an automation — it's the fastest way to catch a broken widget, wrong logo, or mangled subject line before the real delivery.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the automation">
    From the Automations list, click the automation's row or the three-dot menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Test Send">
    Oviond renders the report with the current settings and sends it exactly as the scheduled delivery would.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optionally override recipient">
    By default the test goes to your logged-in email. Use the **Send to** field to target a different address.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check the inbox">
    Verify the subject, sender name, body copy, and — if attached — the PDF looks correct.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Test sends don't count against your monthly email quota and don't appear in [Run History](/automations/history) — they're out-of-band.
</Note>
