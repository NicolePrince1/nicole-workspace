# Bulk Delete

> Remove multiple agency reports at once.

Bulk delete speeds up cleanup when you're pruning a batch of old agency reports — e.g. retired pitch decks or stale internal dashboards.

<Warning>
  Deleted agency reports move to the [Archive](/archive/view) along with all their pages and widgets. They can be restored within the retention window; after that, they're purged permanently. Export anything you need first.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Agency screen">
    From the left sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter selection mode">
    Hover a report card and click the checkbox that appears, or toggle **Select** mode from the toolbar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick the reports">
    Check every report you want to delete.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Delete">
    Click **Delete selected** in the action bar and confirm. Reports move to the Archive.
  </Step>
</Steps>
