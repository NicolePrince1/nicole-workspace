# Bulk Delete

> Remove multiple clients at once from your workspace.

Bulk delete is the fastest way to clean up after offboarding several clients at the same time — or to clear out test clients you no longer need.

<Warning>
  Bulk-deleted clients move to the [Archive](/archive/view) along with all their projects, dashboards, and data-source connections. The clients can be restored from the Archive within the retention window, but anything permanently deleted from the Archive is unrecoverable. Export what you need to keep first.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the client list">
    Go to your home dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable multi-select">
    Hover a client card and click the checkbox that appears, or toggle **Select** mode from the dashboard toolbar. Check every client you want to remove.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Delete">
    With clients selected, click **Delete** in the action bar at the top of the list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review the list in the confirmation dialog, then click **Confirm delete**. Selected clients move to the Archive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Undo a bulk delete

Go to [Archive](/archive/view), select the clients you just removed, and click **Restore**. Their projects and data connections come back intact.
