# Add Theme

> Create a reusable color + font palette for reports and dashboards.

A **theme** is a reusable bundle of visual tokens — primary color, ten chart colors, font, border radius, and shadow style — that can be applied to any project or client.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Themes screen">
    From the left sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click + New Theme">
    Opens the theme editor.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name the theme">
    Use a clear name like *Acme Brand* or *Dark Pitch Deck*.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a primary color">
    Oviond auto-derives a ten-chart color palette from this. You can override any chart color individually.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose typography">
    Pick a font, border radius, and shadow style.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    The theme appears in your Themes library and becomes available in every project's theme picker.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The auto-derived chart palette works for most brands, but if your brand has a mandated full color spec, override each of the ten chart colors manually before saving.
</Tip>
