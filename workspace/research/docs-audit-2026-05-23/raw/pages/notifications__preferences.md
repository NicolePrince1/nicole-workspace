# Preferences

> Choose which notifications trigger email delivery.

**Preferences** are per-user — each team member controls which Oviond events reach their inbox. In-app notifications are always on account-wide; email delivery is the toggle you manage here.

## Event types

| Event                        | Default email | Notes                                                         |
| ---------------------------- | ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Automation failed**        | On            | Email when an automation errors during a scheduled run.       |
| **Integration disconnected** | On            | Email when a data source's OAuth token is revoked or expires. |
| **Team invite**              | On            | Email when a user is invited to your account.                 |
| **Team member removed**      | On            | Email when a team member is removed.                          |
| **PDF export ready**         | Off           | Email when an on-demand PDF is generated.                     |
| **Weekly digest**            | Off           | Summary of activity each Monday.                              |

## How to change preferences

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Notifications → Preferences">
    From the sidebar (or click your avatar → Preferences).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Toggle each event">
    Turn email delivery on or off per event type.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Changes take effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Preferences are per-user — switching them doesn't affect your teammates. In-app notifications (the bell icon) show every event for every user in the account regardless of email preferences.
</Note>
