# Archive Project

> Soft-delete an agency report with full restore within the retention window.

Archiving an agency report removes it from your active Agency list without permanently destroying its pages, widgets, or date-range settings. Restore it from the [Archive](/archive/view) any time before the retention window expires.

## Archive a report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the menu">
    From the Agency screen, click the three-dot menu on the report card, or open the report and use its header menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Archive">
    The report disappears from the active Agency list and moves to the [Archive](/archive/view).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Restore

From the Archive, find the report, click **Restore**, and it reappears in the Agency list with all pages and widgets intact.

## Retention

Archived agency reports older than the retention window (90 days by default) are **permanently purged** by a background job. For long-term storage of reference reports, [duplicate](/agency/duplicate) into a dedicated folder or rename the copy clearly.
