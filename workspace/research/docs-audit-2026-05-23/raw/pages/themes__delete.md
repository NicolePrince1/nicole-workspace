# Delete Theme

> Remove a theme from your library.

Deleting a theme removes it from your Themes library. Projects that used the theme fall back to Oviond's default theme — the projects themselves are untouched.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Themes screen">
    From the left sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the theme menu">
    Click the three-dot menu on the theme card.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Delete">
    Confirm when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Themes from the **Oviond Library** aren't deletable from your account — they're shared and maintained by Oviond.
</Note>

<Warning>
  If the theme is set as a client's **default**, the client's default falls back to Oviond's standard theme. Already-created projects keep their own theme settings.
</Warning>
