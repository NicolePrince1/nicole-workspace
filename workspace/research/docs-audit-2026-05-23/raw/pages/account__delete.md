# Delete Account

> Permanently delete your Oviond account and all associated data.

Deleting an account is **permanent and irreversible**. Every client, project, widget, integration, automation, theme, template, and team member in the account is deleted.

<Warning>
  Account deletion is not a soft-delete. There is no Archive recovery for an entire account. Export any reports you need to keep, and confirm you've ended any active subscription before proceeding.
</Warning>

## Before you delete

* **Export critical reports** — PDF every report you want to retain ([PDF Export](/automations/pdf)).
* **Cancel your subscription** — see [Subscription](/billing/subscription). Deleting an account doesn't automatically cancel billing.
* **Revoke API keys** — integrations using your account's API keys will stop working immediately.
* **Notify your team** — every user in the account loses access the moment you confirm.

## How to delete

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account">
    From the sidebar, click your avatar → **Account Settings**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scroll to Delete Account">
    At the bottom of the page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Delete Account">
    You'll be prompted to type your account name to confirm.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Oviond deletes the account and its data immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Only the account **owner** can delete an account. Admins and other roles don't see the Delete Account button.
</Note>
