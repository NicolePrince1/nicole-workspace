# Bulk Delete

> Remove multiple projects at once.

Bulk delete speeds up cleanup when you're archiving a batch of old reports — for example, rolling over to a new fiscal year or removing drafts after a client offboards.

<Warning>
  Deleted projects move to the [Archive](/archive/view) along with all their pages and widgets. They can be restored within the retention window; after that, they're purged permanently. Export anything you need before proceeding.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Reports screen">
    From the left sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter selection mode">
    Hover a project card and click the checkbox that appears, or toggle **Select** mode from the toolbar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick the projects">
    Check every project you want to delete.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Delete">
    Click **Delete selected** in the action bar and confirm. Projects move to the Archive.
  </Step>
</Steps>
