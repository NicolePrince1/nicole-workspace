# Delete Template

> Remove a template from your library.

Deleting a template removes it from the Templates library. Projects that were created from the template **keep** their pages and widgets — removing the template doesn't touch any project that was already built from it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Templates screen">
    From the left sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the template">
    Use search or filters to locate it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the template menu">
    Click the three-dot menu on the template card.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Delete">
    Confirm when prompted. The template is permanently removed from your library.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Templates in the **Oviond Library** are not deletable from your account — they're shared across every customer and maintained by Oviond.
</Note>
