# Update User

> Change a team member's role, name, or allowed projects.

Only the account **Owner** or **Admin** role can update another user's settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Users">
    Under **Account → Users**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the user's menu">
    Click the three-dot menu next to the user's row.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Edit">
    Opens the user editor.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update the role">
    Change between Owner, Admin, Projects, or Read-only. See [Invite User](/users/invite) for role definitions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update allowed projects (Projects role only)">
    For users with the **Projects** role, pick exactly which projects they can see.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Changes take effect on the user's next page load.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Demoting the **Owner** moves ownership to whoever you promote — you can't leave an account ownerless. To transfer ownership, first make the target user an Admin, then re-invite yourself as Admin and ask the new Owner to demote you.
</Warning>
