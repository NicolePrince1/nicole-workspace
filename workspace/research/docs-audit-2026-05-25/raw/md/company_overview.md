# Company

> Your agency's identity across every report and email.

The **Company** settings live at **Account → Company** and cover everything that represents your agency to clients:

* **Company name** — used in report headers and email footers
* **Logo & favicon** — applied to every report and the sign-in page on custom domains
* **Brand color palette** — generates chart color tokens used across dashboards
* **Support email** — the "reply-to" address shown on delivered reports

For white-label custom domains see **Custom Domains**. For per-report theming see **Templates → Themes**.
