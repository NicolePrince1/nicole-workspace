# Archive Project

> Soft-delete a project with full restore within the retention window.

Archiving a project removes it from your active Reports list without permanently destroying the project's pages, widgets, or date-range settings. You can restore the project from the [Archive](/archive/view) any time before the retention window expires.

## Archive a project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the project menu">
    From the Reports screen, click the three-dot menu on the project card, or open the project and use its header menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Archive">
    The project disappears from the active Reports list and moves to the [Archive](/archive/view).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Restore

From the Archive, find the project, click **Restore**, and it reappears under its original client with all pages and widgets intact.

## Retention

Archived projects older than the retention window (90 days by default) are **permanently purged** by a background job. For long-term storage of reference reports, [duplicate](/projects/duplicate) into a dedicated "Archive" client instead.
