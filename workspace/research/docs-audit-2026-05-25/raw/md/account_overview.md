# Account

> Your Oviond account — the agency workspace that holds every client, project, and team member.

Your account settings let you control everything personal to your login — your display name, language, avatar, and email address — as well as account-level details that apply across your entire Oviond workspace. You can also monitor how much of your plan you've used and, if needed, delete your account entirely.

To reach your account settings, click your avatar or initials in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar, then select **Account Settings**.

## Update your profile

Your profile controls how you appear inside Oviond and, where applicable, in client-facing communications sent from your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile settings">
    Navigate to **Account Settings** and select the **Profile** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update your name">
    Edit the **First name** and **Last name** fields. Your full name appears in the Oviond interface and in team member listings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your language">
    Use the **Language** dropdown to select your preferred display language. This changes the language of the Oviond interface for your login only — it does not affect your teammates' language preferences or what your clients see.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload an avatar">
    Click your current avatar image (or the initials placeholder) and select an image file from your computer. Oviond accepts JPG and PNG files. Your avatar is visible to other team members inside the platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save** to apply your updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Change your email address

Your email address is used to log in to Oviond and to receive platform notifications such as scheduled report confirmations and billing receipts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile settings">
    Go to **Account Settings** and select the **Profile** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your new email address">
    Locate the **Email address** field and type your new email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save**. Oviond may send a verification email to your new address to confirm the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  After changing your email, use the new address the next time you log in.
</Note>

## Account-level settings

Account-level settings apply to your entire Oviond workspace — not just your personal profile. Only the account owner or users with admin permissions can edit these settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account details">
    Go to **Account Settings** and select the **Account** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update your company name">
    Edit the **Company name** field. This name can appear in reports and white-label configurations depending on how your branding is set up.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review other account details">
    Update any other account-level fields shown, such as your website or contact information.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save** to apply the updates across your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## View usage vs. plan limits

Oviond enforces limits on the number of clients, reports (projects), and data integrations you can have active at one time, based on your current plan. You can check your usage at any time to see how much of each limit you've consumed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the usage overview">
    Go to **Account Settings** and select the **Usage** tab (sometimes listed under **Plan & Usage**).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review your usage">
    The usage panel displays the following metrics alongside your plan's limits:

    | Resource               | Description                                                                |
    | ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
    | **Clients**            | The number of client workspaces you've created vs. your plan's maximum.    |
    | **Reports / Projects** | The total number of reports and dashboards across all clients.             |
    | **Integrations**       | The number of active data source connections (e.g., Google Ads, Facebook). |
    | **Team members**       | The number of invited team members vs. your seat limit.                    |
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you're approaching a limit, you can upgrade your plan from **Billing** in the sidebar. See [Plans and billing](/billing/plans) for details on what each plan includes.
</Note>

## Delete your account

Deleting your account permanently removes your Oviond workspace, including all clients, reports, integrations, and team member access. This action cannot be undone.

<Warning>
  Account deletion is irreversible. All your data — clients, reports, dashboards, integrations, and team members — will be permanently deleted. Make sure to export any reports or data you need before proceeding. If you're on a paid plan, deleting your account does not automatically issue a refund; contact [support@oviond.com](mailto:support@oviond.com) before deleting if you have billing questions.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Account Settings** and select the **Account** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the delete option">
    Scroll to the bottom of the page and click **Delete account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Oviond will ask you to confirm by typing your account name or email address. Enter the required text and click **Confirm delete**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your account and all associated data will be removed immediately after confirmation.
