# Delete Automation

> Permanently remove a scheduled delivery.

Deleting an automation removes the schedule and every run-history entry associated with it. The underlying **project** is not affected — only the scheduled delivery.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Automations screen">
    From the left sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the automation menu">
    Click the three-dot menu on the row.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Delete">
    Confirm when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you're temporarily pausing a client's deliveries, toggle **Pause** on the automation instead of deleting. Paused automations keep their history and can be resumed any time. See [List Automations](/automations/list).
</Tip>

## Bulk delete

To remove multiple automations at once, enter **Select mode** on the Automations list, check each automation you want to remove, and click **Delete selected** from the action bar.
